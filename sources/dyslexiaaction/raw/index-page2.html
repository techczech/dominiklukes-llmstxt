Contact us

 

Dyslexia Action Training is now solely a training provider and no longer provides advice, tuition, or dyslexia assessments.
If you are seeking advice, support, tuition, or an assessment for yourself or your child, you can find out more here.

Get in touch

We are experiencing a high volume of enquiries and therefore will aim to get back to you within 3 working days. If you cannot reach us by telephone please email us, providing:

  • a contact telephone number
  • a brief description of your enquiry
  • a contact email address

Please note that our offices are closed on weekends and bank holidays.

In the meantime, don’t forget that many of the answers to queries are on our website.
